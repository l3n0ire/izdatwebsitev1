Bottle Catcher

As the world's populations continues to grow, it'll be more difficult to find places to throw away our trash. One thing everyone can do to help alleviate this problem is by recycling. Bottle catcher aims to promote recycling amoungst adolesence globally in the form of an entertaining mobile game. Bottle catcher teaches players what's recycleable and what's not so they can ensure that all their trash goes into the right bins.
